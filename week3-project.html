Road trip through West Canada

A guide to travelling through beautiful Western Canada via road 🚗 ⛰️ 🏕️

Best seen on four wheels, Canada is blessed with enormous landscapes, wide, wild expanses and roads that wind straight through the middle of it all. To explore lakes, waterfalls, national parks and the jaw-dropping contours of the Rockies in Western Canada, rent a car or take an RV on the road, then eat, sleep and immerse yourself in spectacular scenery. Set off from Pacific-facing Vancouver and head inland towards Calgary. Each windscreen vista will be more spectacular than the last, just watch out for grizzlies! 🐻
